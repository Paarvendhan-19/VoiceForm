## 🌟 THE STAR FEATURE

### Voice Recording Screen ⭐

**When you tap the recording button:**
1. Button changes from mic to stop icon
2. **3 pulse rings appear** and expand outward
3. Rings fade as they expand (opacity 0.3 → 0)
4. Animation repeats every 1.5 seconds
5. "Listening..." text appears
6. Waveform visualization shows audio
7. Tap again to stop → "Processing..." state
8. Auto-advances to next question
